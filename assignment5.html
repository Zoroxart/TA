What are localStorage and sessionStorage?

localStorage and sessionStorage are web storage options provided by modern browsers that allow websites to store data directly in a user's browser.

localStorage stores data with no expiration time, meaning the data remains even after the browser is closed and reopened.

sessionStorage stores data for the duration of the page session. The data is cleared once the browser tab is closed.

These are often used to save user preferences, temporary form inputs, or small pieces of data without needing a server.
